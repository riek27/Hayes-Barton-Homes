Common Questions

What happens after I submit my inquiry?

We'll contact you within 24 hours to schedule a complimentary initial consultation, either in person or virtually, to discuss your project in detail.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No, our initial consultation is completely free. It's an opportunity for us to learn about your vision and for you to get to know our approach.

Do you work with my architect or designer?

Yes, we frequently collaborate with architects and designers. We can either work with your existing team or recommend trusted professionals.
