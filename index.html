About Hayes Barton Homes

Meet Allen Wells

I build dream homes for people looking for niche design touches that mix the traditional with the modern. With more than 30 years experience constructing luxury homes for families and individuals in Raleigh, NC, I've discovered that what my clients value most is a home that is distinctly theirs.

At Hayes Barton Homes, we aren't just experts in building unique custom homes, we're experts in bringing your vision to life. With a civil engineering degree from North Carolina State University and more than 30 years experience building homes in the Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill area, I know homes inside and out.

Every custom home builder has a favorite phase during a build. For me, it's adding interior trim to an almost-finished home. That's when I get to put the 'jewelry' on the house and create those niche touches that make your home uniquely yours.
